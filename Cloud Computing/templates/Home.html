BAIT305C/AMIT3205 Industrial Training Programme

Industrial Training is a compulsory programme that open doors for internship essentials. The programme focuses on student internship attachment, as evidence of work capabilities such as expanding professional network, social values, work quality and ethics.

TARUMT ITP

Industrial Training is a compulsory program that serves as a bridge to vital internship opportunities. It emphasizes practical experience, enabling students to apply their academic knowledge, expand their professional networks, and cultivate essential qualities like work ethics and social values. Through exposure to real-world work environments, students develop skills, gain insights from experienced mentors, and learn to maintain high standards of work quality. This program not only enhances their career prospects but also nurtures their personal and professional growth, making it a crucial component of their education.
