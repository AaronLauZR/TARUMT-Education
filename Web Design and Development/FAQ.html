Shoer Sports FAQ


Q1.Why is the Shoer Sports website created?

Recently, we find out there are quite many people who are very confused about many brands of the sports items, such as top and bottom, shoes, and accessories. Therefore, we, the professionals, decided to create a special website especially for these people by showing the best quality of the sports items in order to be able to solve their major problems.

Q2.Is the Shoer Sports website trustworthy or reliable?

Yes, we are always ready to provide the best customer service to you and keep you satisfied by giving you honest answers and decisions. Besides, we have some customers who are contented with our services. For that reason. They give four to five stars rating to our customer service.

Q3.What can I choose or buy from the Shoer Sports website?

There are four types of sports avaiable in our website, such as basketball, badminton, soccer, and volleyball. Each sport divided in three categories, which are top and bottoms, shoes, and accessories.

Q4.How can I go to the specific website page if I want to buy a basketball shoe?

It is simple, you can hover your cursor on the "All Product". Then, a navigation bar will be appeared below. After that, you may look for the word "basketball" and click the shoe below the word. Therefore, it will direct you to the basketball shoe page.

Q5.Am I able to see all of my chosen items?

Yes, there is a feature called shopping cart, where you can see what you have chosen the items are. In another words, it means you put all the items in a shopping cart and see what you have gotten inside it.

Q6.Where can i get the latest information?

You can get our latest information provided by us on our facebook page or instagram. There are some icons located at the bottom of the page(footer), which can direct you to our official facebook page and instagram. Once you follow these two, you will get to see the latest items updated by our staffs.

Q7.Will there be promotion event organized?

Yes, there will be promotion event organized. It will be held on every last day of the month. Such as 31 January or 30 June. Besides that, the discount for the products will be range from 10% to 25%.

Q8.Will there be new sports categories in the future? If so, what will be them?

This is on our future plan right now. However, we want to see how our customers's responses to our products and our shopping website. If the responses are highly rated. We will think over it to improve our shopping website by adding more products. talking about new products, we may add new products in volleyball, tennis, table tennis, golf, and so. Please look forward it.
